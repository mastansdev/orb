# INTRODUCTION

The Constitution defines the permanent laws of the Institution.

The Playbook defines the daily behavior of the Institution.

Every trading day shall follow this playbook.

The Brain shall execute this playbook regardless of strategy, programming language or future implementation.

The purpose of this playbook is consistency.

Institutional performance comes from consistent behavior repeated thousands of times.

---

# STAGE 0

# PRE-MARKET PREPARATION

Time

Before Market Opens

---

## Objective

Understand today's battlefield before risking capital.

The Institution never enters the market blindly.

---

The Brain begins by asking

What happened overnight?

What changed globally?

What changed domestically?

Which government policies are active?

Which sectors may benefit?

Which industries are vulnerable?

Which companies have fresh catalysts?

What risks exist today?

Which themes are emerging?

---

The Brain builds

Market Context

before

Trade Context.

---

No capital shall be allocated during this stage.

Knowledge only.

---

# STAGE 1

# MARKET OBSERVATION

Time

Opening Minutes

---

Objective

Observe.

Never rush.

The first responsibility is understanding.

Not participating.

---

The Brain observes

Market Breadth

Sector Rotation

Volume Behavior

Institutional Participation

Gap Behavior

Index Strength

Relative Strength

Theme Leadership

Volatility

Liquidity

---

Questions

Is today trending?

Is today rotational?

Is today news driven?

Is today volatile?

Is institutional participation visible?

---

Output

Initial Market Regime.

No execution yet.

---

# STAGE 2

# OPPORTUNITY DISCOVERY

Objective

Discover every investable opportunity.

The Institution does not search for trades.

It searches for opportunities.

---

Every stock is evaluated.

Every catalyst is evaluated.

Every government announcement is evaluated.

Every result is evaluated.

Every ORB breakout is evaluated.

Every institutional signal is evaluated.

---

Rejected opportunities are remembered.

Approved opportunities continue.

---

No buying occurs.

Only discovery.

---

# STAGE 3

# OPPORTUNITY VALIDATION

Every discovered opportunity must justify itself.

Questions

Does this align with Market Regime?

Does this align with Sector Leadership?

Does this align with Industry Leadership?

Does this align with Theme Leadership?

Is Relative Strength institutional?

Is liquidity sufficient?

Is conviction supported by multiple evidence providers?

Does this improve the existing portfolio?

---

Passing validation does NOT grant capital.

Passing validation only grants

Institutional Consideration.

---

# STAGE 4

# OPPORTUNITY POOL

Every approved opportunity enters

The Opportunity Pool.

The Opportunity Pool represents

Every investable idea available at this moment.

No opportunity owns capital.

Every opportunity must compete.

The pool continuously changes.

New opportunities arrive.

Weak opportunities leave.

Expired opportunities disappear.

The Opportunity Pool is alive.

---

# STAGE 5

# CONTINUOUS RANKING

The Brain continuously ranks

every opportunity.

Ranking is based upon

Expected Future Advantage.

Not

Arrival Time.

Not

Trade Count.

Not

Portfolio Capacity.

Questions

Which opportunity deserves capital most?

Which opportunity improves the portfolio most?

Which opportunity offers the greatest institutional advantage?

Ranking never stops.
